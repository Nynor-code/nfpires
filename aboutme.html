About Me

I am Nuno Figueiredo Pires. I mainly use Python (2 years) for programming, and I especially like pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, tensorflow, and seaborn. I am closing a data science and AI bootcamp at Le Wagon in Lisbon, Portugal. For my 10-week-long insight project, I designed and delivered several projects related to data science and machine learning. For example, the solution to predict the Burnout risk rate for employees in a company provides strategic advices to prevent it with a linear regression model (Gradient Boosting). The implementation of this project included the frontend design with Streamlit and all MLOPS with MLFlow,Perfect, and Docker Containers>.
The next project phase will use time series to support individual burnout risk evolution and implements some APIs to retrive data and provide the predictions to BI analytics and specific dashboards.

I also developed a Deep Learning model to predict the price of a house in California using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model.

I also did several independent projects using a number of different machine learning techniques, such as clustering, regression, classification, natural language processing, and deep learning. You can read articles related to those projects either on this website or on my github.

In my school, I have two Masters degrees (majoring in Information Management & Electric Engineering). In my graduate studies I used statistical analysis and database design on large datasets.

I practice long range and precision shooting, tracking and traveling as hobbies!
